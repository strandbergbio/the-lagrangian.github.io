things commonly attributed to conscious experience may be better described as being related to attention, alertness, sense of self, personality, or other cognitive functions.

However, questions about reality can't be resolved by definition, so I want to take an inclusive approach, just dumping in everything that seems vaguely related to "consciousness" in the hopes that they can be sorted into the right bins later on.
